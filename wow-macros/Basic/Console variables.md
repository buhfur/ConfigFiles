## Explanations

## targetNearestDistance

## targetNearestDistanceRadius

## violenceLevel
0-2.<br/>
0 - No hit animations whatsoever<br/>
1 - Hit flashes + green(?) blood (perhaps somewhat lesser extent)<br/>
2 - Hit flashes + red blood<br/>
3 or higher does not change anything, at least not for regular melee.
